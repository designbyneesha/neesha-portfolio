About Me

Who's Neesha, anyway?


Hey there! I'm a kid from California that loves skateboarding, stargazing, poetry, and all things design-related. I've been in the computer science and design industry since I was 14 years old, with work experience including a virtual reality lab, a world-renowned game development studio, and an international robotics company.

I was previously studying Computer Science at the University of Berkeley, California. I ultimately didn't feel satisfied with my experience there, and decided to continue my academic career in Sydney, Australia! You can find me skateboarding in Bondai Beach, installing my art in exhibition shows, or catching the sunset over the cliffs in Vaucluse.
